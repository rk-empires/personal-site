Morning Briefing Automation

A scheduled cron job runs every morning, pulls the previous day's signups from the database, and generates a single digest email. Rather than dumping raw rows, it uses an AI summary step to surface sender intent, cluster contacts by company, and flag higher-intent batches worth prioritizing for outreach — so the day starts with a decision-ready brief instead of a spreadsheet.
